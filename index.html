User Data Deletion

At Zevend, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can easily delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Zevend app
  2. Go to Profile or Settings
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your profile picture
  • All booking history and preferences
  • Data received from Facebook/Google login
  • All personal data associated with your account
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be erased and cannot be recovered.

Timeline

  • Immediate: Your account is deactivated instantly
  • Within 30 days: All your data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Within 90 days: Removed from backup systems

Revoking Social Login Access

After deleting your Zevend account, you may also want to revoke app access from Facebook or Google:

Facebook:

  • Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Find Zevend and click Remove

Google:

  • Go to Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access
  • Find Zevend and click Remove Access
